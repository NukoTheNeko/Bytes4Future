Our Story

One faithfull day Tony's great grandfather Tony decided to open a pizza place, he then left it to Tony's grandad Tony who in turn left it to Tony's Dad Tony who eventually handed it to Tony who will in time leave to his son Tony.

Absolutely nothing of interest happened during that time, and business as always been just about what you'd expect from a pizza place in the middle of New York. No heart touching stories, no rivetting turns of events, no miracles, just absolute normality.

Tony the 2nd came up with the recipe for his patented special pizza during what he himself described, as the most gut wrenching dump he ever took.
